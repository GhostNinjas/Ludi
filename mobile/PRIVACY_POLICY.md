# Privacy Policy for Ludi

**Last Updated:** November 7, 2025

## Introduction

Ludi ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of children and their families. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our educational mobile application for children aged 1-6.

## Children's Privacy

Ludi is designed for children aged 1-6 years old. We are committed to complying with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and other applicable privacy laws.

### Information We Collect

**Locally Stored Information Only:**
- Child's first name (stored locally on your device)
- Child's age (stored locally on your device)
- Child's gender (stored locally on your device)
- Child's interests and preferences (stored locally on your device)
- Game progress and activity data (stored locally on your device)
- App settings and preferences (stored locally on your device)

**We DO NOT:**
- Collect any personally identifiable information on our servers
- Track users across websites or apps
- Share any information with third parties
- Use advertising networks or analytics services
- Require account creation or login
- Store any data in the cloud

### Audio Recording

The app requests microphone permission for:
- Voice-based educational activities
- Speech recognition games

**Audio recordings are:**
- Processed locally on the device only
- Never transmitted to our servers
- Never stored permanently
- Deleted immediately after processing

### Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using:
- AsyncStorage (React Native secure storage)
- Expo SecureStore (for sensitive settings)

### Data Deletion

You can delete all app data at any time by:
1. Going to Settings > Clear All Data within the app, OR
2. Uninstalling the app from your device

Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all locally stored data.

### Third-Party Services

Ludi does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

### Parental Rights

Parents have the right to:
- Review all data stored about their child
- Delete all data at any time
- Refuse to allow further collection of data
- Contact us with questions or concerns

### Changes to Data Collection

If we ever change our data collection practices, we will:
- Update this Privacy Policy
- Notify users within the app
- Request new parental consent if required

### Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect data stored on your device:
- Secure storage mechanisms
- No transmission of sensitive data
- Regular security updates

### Contact Information

For questions or concerns about privacy, please contact us at:

**Email:** privacy@ludi.app
**Website:** https://ludi.app/privacy

### Legal Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with:
- Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) - United States
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) - European Union
- California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) - California, USA
- Other applicable children's privacy laws

### App Store Requirements

This app meets the privacy requirements for:
- Apple App Store (iOS)
- Google Play Store (Android)

### Your Consent

By using Ludi, you consent to this Privacy Policy.
